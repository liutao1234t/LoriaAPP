01

Privacy Approach

Loria is built for creative body-paint workflows, which can involve sensitive photos and expressive personal style. We aim to collect only what is needed to provide the app, protect users, process purchases, respond to support requests, and improve reliability.

This Privacy Policy explains the categories of information we may process when you use Loria. The exact data involved depends on which features you choose, what permissions you grant, whether you create an account, and whether you purchase paid features.

02

Information You Provide

You may provide body photos, face or torso images, design stickers, reference art, prompts, target area selections, style intensity settings, blend mode choices, saved creations, captions, chat questions, profile details, support messages, and consent-related context you choose to include.

Please avoid uploading content you do not need for your creative task. Do not upload another person's image, a minor's image, medical skin condition, identity document, private address, or confidential material unless you have permission and a clear reason to use it in Loria.

03

Information Generated By App Use

We may process technical and usage information such as device type, operating system version, app version, language, region, approximate time of use, feature interactions, crash logs, performance diagnostics, generation status, purchase entitlement status, and security events.

We may also process derived creative metadata, such as selected style category, target body region, export size, saved collection count, and whether a generation completed successfully. This helps operate the app and improve feature quality without needing to know who you are in the real world.

04

Photos, Camera, And Device Permissions

Loria may request access to Photos, Camera, notifications, or other device features only when needed for a function you choose. iOS permission prompts are controlled by Apple. You can grant, deny, or revoke permissions in iOS Settings.

Body photos may reveal sensitive characteristics such as skin tone, tattoos, scars, clothing, body shape, or cultural expression. Loria processes these images to provide body-art editing and generation features you request. We do not use private body photos to identify you as a person.

05

AI Processing

Loria's AI synthesis features are powered by models trained and operated by Loria for body-paint and digital art assistance. The AI uses your selected inputs to generate previews, style ideas, chat responses, and related creative outputs.

The AI feature does not collect personal information for user profiling. Loria does not sell your personal information and does not use AI processing to build an advertising profile about you. AI results may be reviewed through automated safety systems to prevent abuse, protect minors, and enforce content rules.

06

How We Use Information

We use information to provide and personalize app features, process AI generation requests, store drafts and collections, display inspiration content, validate subscriptions or credits, provide customer support, troubleshoot bugs, maintain safety filters, prevent fraud or abuse, enforce terms, comply with law, and understand aggregate product performance.

We may use de-identified or aggregated information to improve Loria's interface, reliability, style taxonomy, and generation quality. We do not treat aggregated information as personal information when it cannot reasonably identify a user.

07

When We Share Information

We may share limited information with service providers that help with hosting, storage, content delivery, analytics, crash reporting, payment validation, customer support, security, and abuse prevention. These providers may process information only to provide services to Loria and must protect it under appropriate obligations.

We may disclose information when required by law, valid legal process, safety emergencies, protection of rights, enforcement of our terms, merger or business transfer, or with your direction. We do not sell personal information.

08

Retention And Deletion

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this Policy, including providing your account features, maintaining saved creations, completing transactions, resolving support issues, meeting legal obligations, preventing abuse, and maintaining backups.

You may delete certain content inside the app where tools are available. You may also request account or data deletion by contacting support@Loria.help. Some records may be retained when required for legal, security, payment, dispute, or backup purposes.

09

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No app, server, model, or transmission method is completely secure, so you should use care when uploading sensitive body photos or sharing generated results.

If you believe your account or content has been exposed, contact us promptly at support@Loria.help with relevant details.

10

Children And Teens

Loria is not directed to children under 13. If local law sets a higher digital consent age, that higher age applies. Users under the age of majority should use Loria only with parent or guardian involvement.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact support@Loria.help and we will take appropriate steps.

11

International Processing

Loria may process information in countries or regions different from where you live. Data protection laws may vary by location, but we use reasonable safeguards for transfers where required.

By using Loria, you understand that information may be processed where Loria or its service providers operate, subject to this Privacy Policy and applicable law.

12

Your Choices And Rights

You can manage app permissions in iOS Settings, manage subscriptions through your Apple ID, edit or delete in-app content where available, and contact us for access, correction, deletion, objection, or other privacy requests that may apply under your local law.

For privacy requests, email support@Loria.help. We may need to verify your request before acting on it, and we may decline or limit requests where permitted by law, such as when we must retain information for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or transaction records.

Questions or requests: support@Loria.help
